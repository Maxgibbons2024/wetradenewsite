The roadmap

From zero experience to funded trader, in five steps.

We don't teach discretionary trading. We teach a mechanical system — rules you can test, repeat, and execute without emotion — and we walk you through the funded-account process end to end.

Choose your program
  1. 01Month 1

    Learn the mechanical rules

    A fixed, rules-based system: X + Y + Z = place trade. No subjective setups, no indicators to second-guess. If the conditions are met, you take the trade.

  2. 02Months 2–3

    Build confidence through backtesting

    Demo trading and structured backtesting until the rules become reflex. Journal every trade, review with your mentor, refine your execution.

  3. 03Months 3–4

    Pass a funded-account evaluation

    We walk you through a 1-step evaluation with our funding partner. Hit the profit target, respect the drawdown rules, unlock the capital.

  4. 04Months 4–7

    Receive your first payouts

    Trade the funded account. First payouts typically land in months 4–7 at 3–5% on six-figure funding — paid out on a regular schedule.

  5. 05Month 7+

    Scale up your funding

    As you build a track record, you qualify for larger accounts. Bigger funding, bigger payouts, same mechanical rules.
