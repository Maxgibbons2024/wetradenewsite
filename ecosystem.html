EdgeTrack

EdgeTrack is a professional-grade trading journal application built specifically for serious traders. It tracks every aspect of your trading, surfaces the data that matters, and helps you identify patterns in your performance.

Built by traders, for traders — EdgeTrack is the tool that turns raw trade data into actionable insight. It is used by WeTrade students and available to all traders.

  • Detailed trade logging with automatic calculations
  • Performance analytics and statistical breakdowns
  • Pattern recognition to identify strengths and weaknesses
  • Clean, professional interface designed for daily use

Prophouse

Prophouse is a proprietary trading firm within the WeTrade ecosystem. It represents the end goal for many students: the opportunity to trade with real capital, backed by a firm that understands the methodology they were trained on.

For students who have developed consistency through the WeTrade programme, Prophouse provides funded trading opportunities. Over 50 WeTrade students have already gone through this pathway successfully.

  • Funded trading challenges for qualified traders

  • 50+ WeTrade students already funded
