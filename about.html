How It Works

The WeTrade approach.

Rules-Based Trading

At its core, WeTrade teaches a rules-based approach to the markets. This means every decision — from identifying a setup to managing a trade — follows a defined set of criteria. This removes emotion, builds consistency, and creates a repeatable edge.

Market Structure & Price Action

The methodology is built on an understanding of market structure and price action. Students learn to read the markets as they are, not through the lens of lagging indicators or overly complex systems. The result is a clean, direct way of understanding where price is likely to move and why.

Risk Management First

Before any student learns to enter a trade, they learn to manage risk. This is non-negotiable. WeTrade's approach treats capital preservation as the foundation of long-term profitability. Every trade has predefined risk parameters before it is taken.

Continuous Development

Trading is a skill, not a one-time course. WeTrade's model reflects this through ongoing live analysis, weekly webinars, community engagement, and mentorship reviews that continue long after the initial curriculum is completed.
